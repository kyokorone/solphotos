about solphotos & the photographer.

what is solphotos?

SolPhotos is dedicated to capturing moments in everyday life. Whether it's the streets of Dumaguete, a trip to another town, special events, or a portrait of a new friend, photography has the power to preserve such kinds of memories forever. With this in mind, this site is a collection of these moments, for keepsakes.

who is solphotos?

Angela Sol is a student photographer living in Dumaguete City, Negros Oriental. Currently taking up a Bachelors degree in Information and Technology at Silliman University, she has been a dedicated member of The Silliman University Camera Club since 2022. She's experienced in event and street photography, but has a soft spot for ezperimental photography... although she has yet to dive into it more.

Angela grew up in Greer, South Carolina, USA, moving to Dumaguete City in 2015 at 11 years old. Her love for photography began at a young age, starting with portraits of her dolls in the backyard of her home. During her junior high years, she took a summer photography class under the mentorship of established Dumaguete photographer, Urich Calumpang. ever since, she has been practicing and ever-growing her skills in photography, especially through the Silliman University Camera Club and its passionate community.
